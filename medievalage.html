Medieval Era ⚔️


The Medieval Era, often called the Middle Ages, was a time of knights, castles, kingdoms, and chivalry. Art, architecture, and literature flourished under the rule of kings and queens.


"A knight is sworn to valor, his heart guided by honor and duty."
